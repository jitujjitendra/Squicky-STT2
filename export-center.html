Export Center - Help
  • Select Options: Choose what to include (timestamps, speakers, title, metadata).
  • Choose Format: Click Export on any format card (TXT, PDF, SRT, VTT, JSON, CSV, DOCX).
  • Preview: Click format tabs to preview how the export will look.
  • Export All: Click "Export All Formats" to download all at once.
  • PDF: Opens print dialog - use "Save as PDF" in the print window.
  • Source: Data comes from your last recording or Transcript Studio edits.
